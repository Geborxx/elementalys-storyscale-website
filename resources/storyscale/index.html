What StoryScale Does

StoryScale helps Agile teams estimate a bounded slice of Jira work by comparing stories relative to each other instead of debating isolated point values. It imports issues, checks readiness signals, builds a live complexity ranking, calibrates estimates, and publishes approved Story Points back to Jira.

For teams

Reduce sprint planning complexity while keeping the final decision visible.

For Jira admins

Forge-first implementation with explicit permissions and controlled write-back.
